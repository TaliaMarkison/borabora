Bora Bora

Bora Bora is a tropical paradise with lush tropical slopes, valleys, and hills. White-sand beaches extenuate the bright blue sparkling waters where colored fish of all sorts live in the coral gardens.

Bora Bora is a tiny litte island located in the South Pacific Ocean. It is near other islands like the French Polynesia and Tahaa.
